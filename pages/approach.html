Participatory Applied Research

Our understanding of digital media literacy in the 21st century is limited. For the benefit of students, educators, and policy makers alike—we will engage in participatory applied research to discover innovative ways to make every young person a critical user of digital media. The Virtual Platform

Opportunities for communication, exchange and collaboration in the field of media literacy have been limited. We're building an online platform and partnerships where leading thinkers, organizations, and community members can come together to share information, insights and best practices. This will be an ongoing way to keep the community connected and coordinated beyond conferences, and beyond annual conferences and regional events.

The platform will be a global space for members of the field to:

Showcase resources and best practices

Share relevant events and initiatives

Offer webinars and training sessions
