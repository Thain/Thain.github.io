When it comes to helping you pick up creative skills, our philosophy is simple:
we want you to learn by doing.

That's why we deliver workshops, mentorship, and hands-on learning experiences to young people who want to learn video, design, web development, and marketing skills.

We teach workshops, so you can wrap your head around the basics.


Our creative team leads weekly workshops on storytelling, videography, design, and more. Check out the workshops that we've got coming up in the next month.

View our calendar.

2 / 3

We provide mentorship, because a little makes all the difference.


We'l connect you to one of our creatives so that you can shadow them while they work on an ongoing campaign. They can answer your questions and give you a little guidance on where to go next.

Get acquainted with some of our team members.

3 / 3

Eventually, we want you to take the wheel.


After a couple workshops and some shadowing — we'll connect you, and a couple of other learners, to an organization that needs your support. At that point, what yo ucreate is up to you (but we'll always be around to help).

See some of the work that our learners have put together.

All of this is built around community.

We know that connection and collaboration are at the essence of creativity. That's why we hold weekly community meetings at Building 21 at 6:30 pm on Thursdays. These are spaces to connect, discuss, critique, and collaborate. Come around.
